Introduction to Hello Kitty :

❤︎Welcome to the world of Hello Kitty! Created in 1974 by the Japanese company Sanrio, Hello Kitty is more than just a cute face
❤︎she’s a symbol of friendship and happiness for fans worldwide. Known for her pink bow and lovable personality,
❤︎Hello Kitty has inspired generations with her kindness and playful spirit!

History of Hello Kitty:

♡Hello Kitty was created in 1974 by Yuko Shimizu for Sanrio. Since then, she has become one of the most famous fictional characters in the world. Originally designed to appeal to young girls, Hello Kitty has grown to become a cultural icon loved by fans of all ages. She started on small items like coin purses and has expanded into fashion, food, and even amusement parks!

Character Bio:

♡Hello Kitty lives in London with her family, including her parents, George and Mary, and her twin sister, Mimmy. She’s a cheerful girl who loves baking cookies, playing piano, and making friends. Even though she doesn’t have a mouth, Hello Kitty speaks from the heart!
